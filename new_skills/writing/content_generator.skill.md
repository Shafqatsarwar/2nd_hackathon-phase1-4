# Content Generator Skill

## Purpose
Automatically generates high-quality written content based on topic, style, and audience requirements.

## Functionality
- Generates blog posts, articles, social media content, and marketing copy
- Adapts tone and style to match brand guidelines
- Incorporates SEO best practices and keyword optimization
- Creates content calendars and topic suggestions
- Ensures grammatical accuracy and readability

## Input
- Content topic or brief
- Target audience description
- Desired length and format
- Brand voice and style guidelines
- Keywords or themes to include

## Output
- Draft content ready for review
- SEO optimization suggestions
- Alternative headlines and angles
- Content calendar recommendations
- Readability analysis

## LOC
Approximately 300-400 lines of code

## Measurement
- Time reduction: From 1-2 hours manual writing to 10-15 minutes automated generation
- Consistency: Maintains brand voice across all content
- Efficiency: Multiple variations can be generated simultaneously

## Files
- `content_generator.py`
- `templates/content_templates.json`
- `utils/seo_optimizer.py`
