# Competitive Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Automatically gathers and analyzes competitor information from various online sources to create comprehensive competitive intelligence reports.

## Functionality
- Scrapes competitor websites for product offerings and pricing
- Analyzes competitor social media presence and engagement
- Compares features against your products
- Generates SWOT analysis for each competitor
- Creates visual comparison charts

## Input
- Competitor company names/URLs
- Your product features list
- Analysis parameters and focus areas
- Frequency of monitoring

## Output
- Competitive intelligence report
- Feature comparison matrix
- Pricing analysis table
- Market positioning assessment
- Strategic recommendations

## LOC
Approximately 250-350 lines of code

## Measurement
- Time reduction: From 2-3 hours manual research to 15-30 minutes automated analysis
- Coverage: Comprehensive data collection vs. manual sampling
- Accuracy: Consistent methodology across all competitors
