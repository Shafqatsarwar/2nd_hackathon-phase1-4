# Improved Competitive Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Advanced competitive intelligence gathering and analysis with AI-powered insights and predictive capabilities.

## Functionality
- Automated web scraping and data collection from multiple sources
- Natural language processing for sentiment and positioning analysis
- Predictive modeling for competitor behavior and market moves
- Advanced SWOT and Porter's Five Forces analysis
- Real-time monitoring and alert system for competitive moves
- Integration with CRM and sales data for impact assessment

## Input
- Competitor websites, social media accounts, press releases
- Industry reports and market data
- Internal sales and customer feedback
- Custom monitoring parameters and alerts

## Output
- Executive-level competitive intelligence dashboard
- Detailed competitor profiling with strategic recommendations
- Predictive analysis of competitor next moves
- Market positioning heat maps
- Automated alerts for significant competitive activities
- Quarterly competitive trend reports

## LOC
Approximately 500-700 lines of code

## Measurement
- Time reduction: From 4-6 hours manual research to 20-30 minutes automated analysis
- Coverage: 10x more data sources vs. manual monitoring
- Accuracy: AI-powered pattern recognition vs. human bias
- Proactivity: Predictive insights vs. reactive analysis
